# FIWB: Institutional Intelligence & Digital Twin

FIWB is a state-of-the-art **Neural Academic Assistant** designed to act as a student's **Digital Twin**. It bridges the gap between institutional course materials (Classroom, Moodle) and personal workspace context (Gmail, Drive), providing an elite Socratic mentoring experience.

---

## üß† Core Intelligence Pillars

### 1. The Digital Twin (Long-Term Memory)
- **Neural Synthesis**: Every interaction is analyzed to extract strengths, knowledge gaps, and communication preferences.
- **Perpetual Context**: The AI "grows" with you, remembering past discussions and evolving its teaching style.
- **Memory Bank**: Synthesized insights are stored in a dedicated high-fidelity memory vault for long-term recall.

### 2. Neural Assistant (Personal Workspace)
- **Gmail Primary Inbox Sync**: Proactively monitors your inbox to extract assignments, appointments, and personal context.
- **Google Drive Integration**: Indexes PDFs, documents, and notes directly into your cognitive workspace.
- **Task & Deadline Awareness**: Automatically identifies commitments from emails and references them naturally during tutoring.

### 3. Socratic Institutional Mentor
- **Institutional Sync**: Deep integration with **Google Classroom** and **Moodle**.
- **Evidence-Based Mentoring**: Quotes directly from your course materials and academic notes.
- **Socratic Method**: Instead of just giving answers, the AI guides students through complex concepts using inquiry-based learning.
